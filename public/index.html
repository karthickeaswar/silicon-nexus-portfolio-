Simulation & Silicon

Student Researcher

I still remember the exact moment I stopped thinking of circuits as textbook problems. Standing in the RF lab, holding a small FR4 board I had fabricated — a 6G Edge-Fed 2×2 Array Antenna designed in CST Studio. When the S11 dipped below −10 dB right where my simulation predicted, I understood — the gap between a simulation and a working device is where real engineering lives.

That conviction carried me into research with a TNSCST-funded grant, industry experience at RWS IP Solutions, and a multi-domain technical foundation spanning analog RF, digital VLSI, and RISC-V processor design.
